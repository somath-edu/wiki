THE LIVING ART

HAKORIUM is Korea's specialist gallery for rare Platycerium and bioactive terrariums. Expert curation, species-matched care, and consultation — all in one place.

SPECIALIST CURATION

The Platycerium is an epiphytic fern native to tropical rainforests, anchored to trees and drawing moisture and nutrients directly from the surrounding air. Specimens developed over decades acquire a structural individuality that cannot be replicated — no two are identical. HAKORIUM works directly with specialist growers in Thailand, Borneo, and the Philippines to source rare species including P. Ridleyi, P. Superbum, and P. Iregoi, varieties seldom available in the Korean market. Every specimen is hand-mounted on natural timber boards using a proprietary substrate blend and stabilised sphagnum moss, and supplied in settled condition. hakorium.com

BIOACTIVE TERRARIUM DESIGN

HAKORIUM terrariums are engineered on a four-layer substrate system that mirrors the cross-section of a natural forest floor. Volcanic pumice provides drainage, activated charcoal acts as a pH buffer, akadama hosts beneficial microbial activity, and a moss surface layer regulates humidity through condensation and absorption. Within this system, water cycles continuously, microorganisms process organic matter, and the environment self-regulates without external intervention. This methodology, known in botanic science as bioactive terrarium design, is what HAKORIUM introduced to Korean consumers as a professional practice.

SPECIALIST CONSULTATION & SHOWROOM

HAKORIUM operates a dedicated Platycerium showroom designed to support informed purchasing decisions. The display environment — lighting, ventilation, and layout — is configured to reflect real residential conditions, allowing visitors to evaluate each specimen as it would appear in their own home. Consultations are structured around the visitor's living environment, natural light conditions, and daily routine. The result is a one-to-one specialist recommendation process that matches plant characteristics to spatial requirements. Floor-standing timber mounts, wall-hung panels, and shelf configurations are available for in-person assessment, and installation services are included.

SPECIALIST AFTERCARE

The long-term health of a Platycerium depends on species-appropriate management of hydration, fertilisation, and air circulation. HAKORIUM provides structured aftercare support with every purchase: a species-specific watering schedule, seasonal fertilisation guidelines, and a mounting-condition inspection checklist, all delivered as a printed care guide alongside the specimen. Purchasers additionally receive access to one-to-one consultation with a dedicated plant manager and one complimentary in-home inspection per year. The relationship with HAKORIUM continues well beyond the point of purchase.
